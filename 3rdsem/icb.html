Innovation: Uber
Uber was founded by Garrett Camp and Travis Kalanick, in March 2009. Once the founders were in Paris to attend a LeWeb, an annual tech conference in 2007, and after the conference the duo was unable to find a cab in the mid of winter night, and at last the duo had to spent $800 hiring a private driver, that's when the idea came to them that sharing the cost with people could make it affordable, but and the morphed into Uber. following that they launched a beta version of their app in march 2010 with only 3 cars, and finally the official launch in 2011. Initially, Uber only allowed users to book luxury cars.
As the company came to focus, the field of service increased, initially starting from 3 cars they expanded to more than hundreds cars in a year.
